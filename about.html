Organisation Overview

Cozziano is a South African streetwear brand that focuses on delivering modern, stylish, and affordable fashion to young people. The brand was created to represent urban culture and individuality through clothing.

Mission

To provide high-quality streetwear that allows individuals to express themselves confidently.

Vision

To become one of the leading streetwear brands in South Africa and expand across Africa.

Target Market

Youth aged 16–30 who are interested in fashion, trends, and street culture.
